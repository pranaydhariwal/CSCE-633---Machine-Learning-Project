
Sentiment analysis is a standard text classification problem in Natural Language Processing.


########################################  SST-2  ######################################
The Stanford Sentiment Treebank consists of sentences extracted from movie reviews and human annotations of their sentiment. Given a sentence, the task is to determine the sentiment of the sentence (positive or negative).

Label: 0-negative, 1-positive

More info: https://nlp.stanford.edu/sentiment/index.html
